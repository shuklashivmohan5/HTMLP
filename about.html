About
Vivek Menon

Vivek Menon is an Indian wildlife conservationist, environmental commentator, and author. He is known for his extensive work in wildlife conservation and his efforts to raise awareness about India's rich biodiversity. Menon has written several books on Indian wildlife, including field guides and comprehensive works on specific species. He is also involved in various conservation initiatives and organizations in India, working to protect endangered species and their habitats. Menon's contributions to wildlife conservation have earned him recognition both nationally and internationally.


Random Facts About Vivek Menon

  1. Menon is the founder and executive director of the Wildlife Trust of India (WTI), a leading Indian organization dedicated to the conservation and protection of wildlife and habitats.
  2. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Zoology and a Master's degree in Environmental Science.
  3. Menon has authored several books on Indian wildlife, including "Indian Mammals: A Field Guide", "Mammals of India", and "The Secret Lives of Indian Mammals".
  4. He has held various leadership roles in conservation organizations and initiatives, including serving as a member of the IUCN Species Survival Commission and the Asian Rhino Specialist Group.
  5. Menon's work in wildlife conservation has been recognized with awards such as the Carl Zeiss Wildlife Conservation Award and the Sanctuary Lifetime Service Award.
  6. He is actively involved in advocating for wildlife conservation through public speaking engagements, media appearances, and writing articles for various publications.
  7. Menon has contributed to scientific research in the field of wildlife conservation, particularly focusing on the conservation status and ecology of Indian mammal species.
  8. He has been involved in numerous conservation campaigns and initiatives aimed at raising awareness about wildlife conservation issues and promoting sustainable practices.
  9. Menon is also known for his photography of Indian wildlife, capturing stunning images of various species in their natural habitats.
  10. He is passionate about educating the public, especially youth, about the importance of wildlife conservation and environmental stewardship through workshops, seminars, and educational programs.
